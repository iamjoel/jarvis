# Jarvis
Live an easier life with the help of Jarvis. Spend more time on what really matters instead of mundane tasks.

Jarvis is an agent powered by LLMs.

## Why life can feel overwhelming
### 1. The Entropy of Existence
![](./resource/order-to-disorder.png)

Life naturally moves from order to disorder. Life requires constant energy to keep things in order.[^1]


### 2. Unhappiness
$$
Happiness = Reality(Ability) / Expectations(Desire)
$$

When **Reality** (your current situation) is less than **Expectations** (what you want), you feel unhappy.

![](./resource/desire-and-ability.png)  
**Desire** grows faster than **Ability**. When your ability increases (you get a promotion, you learn a new skill), your brain quickly treats this new level as the "baseline."[^2]

Also, **social media and advertising** constantly raise our expectations, which can make us feel inadequate.


### 3. Stress from rapidly advancing technology
As society becomes more technologically advanced and efficient, individuals often feel more pressured, anxious, and exhausted.

![](./resource/attention-fatigue.png)  
**Attention Fatigue**: Our brains process more information in a single day than our ancestors did in a lifetime. This leads to what clinicians call "digital noise" stress.

![](./resource/ai-replacement.png)  
**AI replacement**: As AI becomes more capable, many people worry about job security and the future of work. 


## How to respond
Use Jarvis to:
