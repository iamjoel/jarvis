# Few shot
**Best for:** the task is complex or when you want to guide the model towards a specific style or format.

Sometimes, Give the model some examples can significantly improve the quality of the output.

## How to Use
Provide 2-5 examples of input-output pairs that demonstrate the desired behavior. Then, give the model a new input and ask for the corresponding output.

Attention: If the examples are too many, it may cause overfitting.
